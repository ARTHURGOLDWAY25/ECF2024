Green Transportation

Help Cut Carbon

For those who have the option, traveling to Safari Heaven Zoo using alternative methods helps reduce your carbon footprint.

Busses

The 22, 36, 151, and 156 buses stop along the western edge of the zoo.

Trains

The Brown and Purple Lines stop at the Armitage station and the Red Line stops at the Fullerton station. Both stations are accessible and located about one mile west of the zoo.

To learn more about taking the CTA, call 312-836-7000 or use the CTA’s trip-planner.

Bicycles

Bicycle racks are located near East Gate, West Gate, North Gate, and Café Brauer Gate, with additional racks along Cannon and Stockton drives. There are also several nearby Divvy stations.

Carpooling is another great way to reduce your environmental impact while visiting Safari Heaven Zoo. We encourage all visitors to consider eco-friendly transportation options and join us in our efforts to protect the environment.

For more information on sustainable travel and other initiatives at Safari Heaven Zoo, visit our website or contact our guest services team. Together, we can make a difference!
