shop at the zoo

The Gift Shop

10 a.m. – 5 p.m.

Browse unique, nature-inspired items for kids and adults, including apparel, handcrafted jewelry, travel gear, souvenirs, games, puzzles, educational toys, books, and a huge variety of plush animals. Every purchase helps keep the zoo free and open to all while advancing world-class animal care, global wildlife conservation, and local learning opportunities.

Safari Heaven Zoo and AAA members get a 10% discount on all gift shop purchases.

Discover a delightful selection of eco-friendly products and gifts at our shop, ranging from reusable water bottles and tote bags to organic skincare and sustainable home decor. Each item is carefully curated to inspire a love for nature and promote environmental consciousness. By shopping with us, you contribute to the zoo's mission of providing exceptional animal care, supporting wildlife conservation efforts, and fostering educational programs for all ages.

dine at the zoo

Lincoln Park Zoo and AAA members receive a 10% discount at all zoo dining locations.

The Patio at Café Brauer

Vegetarian, Gluten-Free*

Open Seasonally

Weekdays, 11 a.m.–6 p.m. & Weekends, 10 a.m.–7 p.m..

The Patio at Café Brauer offers panoramic views of Nature Boardwalk, a restored native ecosystem, and soaring skyscrapers. Dine on savory small plates and specialty burgers. Sip cocktails, craft-brewed ales, and wine.

*Gluten-free options are not listed on the menu. If you have allergic or dietary restrictions, please inform your server and we will do our best to accommodate your request.
